# Keurig Dr Pepper (keurig-dr-pepper)
Keurig Dr Pepper is a leading North American beverage company formed by the 2018 merger of Keurig Green Mountain and Dr Pepper Snapple Group. It operates a portfolio of more than 125 owned, licensed, partner, and allied brands across hot and cold beverages, including Keurig single-serve brewing systems and brands such as Dr Pepper, 7UP, Snapple, Mott's, Bai, Green Mountain Coffee Roasters, and Peet's Coffee.
